Ingredients


  • Pizza Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Pepperoni Slices
  • Mushrooms
  • Bell Peppers
  • Black Olives
  • Fresh Basil
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic (optional)

Steps to create this masterpiece


  1. Preheat your oven to [temperature].
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Spread a generous layer of tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  4. Sprinkle a generous amount of mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.
  5. Arrange your favorite toppings, such as pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, black olives, and fresh basil, on top of the cheese.
  6. Optionally, drizzle a bit of olive oil and add minced garlic for extra flavor.
  7. Carefully transfer the pizza to a preheated oven and bake until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Remove from the oven, let it cool for a moment, slice, and enjoy your homemade pizza masterpiece!
