Ingredients


  • Spaghetti Noodles
  • Ground Beef or Meat Substitute
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Italian Herbs
  • Crushed Red Pepper (optional)
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Fresh Basil (optional)
  • Olive Oil

Steps to Create this Delightful Dish


  1. Boil water and cook spaghetti noodles according to package instructions; drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, saut√© onions and garlic in olive oil until aromatic; add ground beef or meat substitute and cook until browned.
  3. Pour in the tomato sauce, sprinkle with Italian herbs, and add crushed red pepper for a hint of spice; let it simmer.
  4. Toss the cooked noodles into the flavorful sauce, ensuring each strand is coated.
  5. Serve the noodle dish hot, garnished with freshly grated Parmesan cheese and a sprinkle of fresh basil if desired.
  6. Pair with a glass of your favorite beverage, and savor the perfection of your homemade noodle creation!
